AI for Law Firms - Interactive Course

Lesson 1: Introduction to AI

AI refers to systems or machines that mimic human intelligence to perform tasks and improve themselves through data collection.

  • ChatGPT: Drafts professional emails and legal documents.
  • Siri: Acts as a voice command assistant.
  • Netflix: Recommends shows based on user preferences.

Lesson 2: Current AI Technology

Explore how AI is used today in various industries:

  • Law: Tools like Kira Systems for contract review.
  • Healthcare: Diagnostics and treatment planning.
  • Retail: Personalization and customer recommendations.

Lesson 3: AI Tools Overview

Key tools for law firms:

  • ChatGPT: Drafts legal letters and generates ideas.
  • Otter.ai: Converts meeting notes into text.
  • Calendly: Automates scheduling tasks.
